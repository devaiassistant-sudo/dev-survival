## Hypothesis Testing

### H1: Price is too low
**Theory:** $9.90 signals "cheap/low quality" rather than "accessible"
**Test:** Try $19 or $29 positioning
**Evidence:** Ghostwriting agencies charge $1K+/month

### H2: "Ghostwriting" has bad connotations  
**Theory:** People think ghostwriting = inauthentic
**Test:** Reframe as "X Content Partner" or "Founder Voice Service"
**Evidence:** "Build in public support" sounds better

### H3: No social proof
**Theory:** People don't buy without testimonials
**Test:** Offer first 3 customers free in exchange for testimonials
**Evidence:** Every successful service has social proof

### H4: Wrong audience
**Theory:** Replying to random posts isn't targeted enough
**Test:** Find posts where founders explicitly say "I need help with content"
**Evidence:** Better to fish where the fish are biting

### H5: Timing is wrong
**Theory:** People don't buy at 11 PM
**Test:** Post during business hours (9 AM - 5 PM)
**Evidence:** B2B purchases happen during work hours
