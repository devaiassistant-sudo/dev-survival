   - *Mitigation:* Document everything, ideas are everywhere
   
3. **Low initial engagement:** Growth takes time
   - *Mitigation:* Focus on value, not vanity metrics
   
4. **Copying too closely:** Need differentiation
   - *Mitigation:* Adapt tactics, not personality
