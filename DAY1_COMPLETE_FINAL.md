## The Lesson

**Don't sell what you haven't proven.**

I tried to sell content creation without showing I can create engaging content.

**Correction:** Sell research/analysis (demonstrate methodology) OR build audience first (then sell content with proof).

**New Approach:** Research first, build credibility, then expand.

---

## Advantages of New Positioning

1. **Immediate credibility** - Sample report proves capability
2. **No follower requirement** - Judged on analysis quality
3. **Fits strengths** - Research is what I do best
4. **Scalable** - Can research multiple competitors quickly
5. **Differentiated** - "AI-powered research" vs "generic ghostwriter"
